Teaching Philosophy

I believe in fostering a learning environment that emphasizes critical thinking, collaboration, and the power of storytelling in understanding labor and social movements.

My approach integrates oral history, historical context, and interactive methods to engage students in meaningful inquiry.

Teaching in Action

Courses Taught

Introduction to U.S. Labor History

Explores labor movements from the Gilded Age to today, analyzing historical and contemporary issues in organizing.

African American and Latinx Histories

Examines intertwined histories of Black and Latinx communities, migration, and resistance in the U.S.
