About My Journey

As a first-generation Mexican and Salvadorean from Humboldt Park, Chicago, I witnessed the challenges of working-class life and the strength found in solidarity.

Today, as a rising PhD student in Cornell’s ILR School, my ethnographic, bilingual research amplifies marginalized voices through community-engaged partnerships.
