My Work, My Roots

I am a Ph.D. student at Cornell University's School of Industrial and Labor Relations. My research and activism center on worker dignity, collective power, and migrant justice. From delivering pizzas to delivering lectures, my journey reflects resilience and commitment to social transformation.

Worker Dignity. Collective Power. Migrant Justice.

I research how unions and worker centers empower marginalized workers. My work explores how dignity is cultivated in workplaces marked by linguistic, ethnic, and legal precarity.
